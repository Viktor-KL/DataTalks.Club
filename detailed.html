Question and Answer

01

Is it going to be live? When?

It is, on Mondays at 17:00 CET.

02

Will it be recorded?

Yes, everything will be recorded and you can watch the recording when it's convenient for you.

03

What if I miss a session?

The sessions are recorded, and you can ask questions in Slack.

04

How much theory will you cover?

The bare minimum. The focus is more on practice, and we'll cover the theory only on the intuitive level. E.g. we won't derive the gradient update rule for logistic regression (there are other great courses for that), but we'll cover how to use logistic regression and make sense of the results.

05

I don't know math. Can I take the course?

Yes! We'll cover some linear algebra in the course, but in general, there will be very few formulas, mostly code.

06

How long will the sessions be?

Not longer than one hour each.

07

I filled the form, but haven't received a confirmation email. Is it normal?

Yes. We process the sign-ups once per week. To make sure you don't miss anything, join the #course-ml-zoomcamp channel.
